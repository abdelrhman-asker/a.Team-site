Two ways to build with A.Team

A.Teams plug into your organization in two main ways to empower agile innovation - augmenting current teams or tackling greenfield opportunities.

Supercharge Your Team

You have a team of all-stars. But to get your product off the ground, you need additional capacity or niche expertise. Add high-quality firepower to accelerate your product development or inject missing skills into your team.

Example: Team Augmentation
Internal Product Mgr.
Internal Full-Stack
Internal Back-End

A.Team Designer

Build a Team From Scratch

You have an idea or a plan for a product. But you need the team to deliver your vision. Build an A.Team from scratch as your core product team — or as an additional team to work on additional lines of business (while keeping your core team focused).

HOW IT WORKS
Let's build your dream team

1

Plan your Team

Tell us what you're building and what roles, skills, and experience you're looking for. And estimate your start date and how long you expect your mission to last.

2

Review your team proposal

After your team request, we'll send you your team proposal within a week. Make comments — and a team formation specialist will help you make team revisions.

3

Deploy & start building

Once you're happy with your team, we'll give you your own team dashboard where you can sign contracts, officially deploying your mission!

WEB3

Building a “remote control” app to interact with hardware crypto wallets

Ledger, a hardware crypto wallet company, built an A.Team to create a companion app that acts as a remote control for Ledger Live—the homebase for all things crypto management. With A.Team’s building expertise, a browser extension that enables investors to interact with web3 services on any blockchain was brought to life, providing the high level security and reliable communication necessary for crypto transactions.
