Two ways to build with A.Team

A.Teams plug into your organization in two main ways to empower agile innovation - augmenting current teams or tackling greenfield opportunities.

Supercharge Your Team

You have a team of all-stars. But to get your product off the ground, you need additional capacity or niche expertise. Add high-quality firepower to accelerate your product development or inject missing skills into your team.

Example: Team Augmentation

Build a Team From Scratch

You have an idea or a plan for a product. But you need the team to deliver your vision. Build an A.Team from scratch as your core product team — or as an additional team to work on additional lines of business (while keeping your core team focused).
